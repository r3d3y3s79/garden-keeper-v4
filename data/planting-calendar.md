## How to use this calendar

- Each zone has a **12-month grid** showing what to plant, transplant, and harvest
- **Baseline**: All planting windows are anchored to USDA Zone 6 (temperate)
- **Colder zones** (USDA 3-5): planting windows shift **later** by 1-2 months
- **Warmer zones** (USDA 7-10): planting windows shift **earlier** and the season **extends**
- **Tropical Australia**: planting follows **wet/dry seasonal pattern** rather than frost cycle

**Sources**: USDA Plant Hardiness Zone Map, Australian Bureau of Meteorology climate zones, standard seed-packet planting windows (Burpee, Botanical Interests, Johnny's Selected Seeds).

### 🌿 Basil
**Category:** Herb

_Frost-tender. Pinch tips to encourage bushy growth._

**Category:** Legume

_Bush and pole types. Direct sow after soil reaches 15°C. Inoculate seeds._

### 🫜 Beet
**Category:** Root

_Direct sow. Thin to 10cm. Both roots and greens are edible._

### 🥦 Broccoli
**Category:** Brassica

_Cool-season. Spring and fall crops. Cut main head then side shoots keep coming._

### 🥬 Cabbage
**Category:** Brassica

_Cool-season. Heirloom storage varieties keep through winter._

### 🥕 Carrot
**Category:** Root

_Direct sow only — transplants fail. Loose, stone-free soil. Thin seedlings to 5cm._

### 🥬 Cauliflower
**Category:** Brassica

_Cool-season. More finicky than broccoli. Blanch heads by tying leaves over them._

### 🥬 Chard
**Category:** Leafy

_Heat-tolerant alternative to spinach. Cut outer leaves; plant keeps producing._

### 🌿 Cilantro
**Category:** Herb

_Cool-season — bolts fast in heat. Sow successively. Let some flower for coriander seed._

### 🌽 Corn
**Category:** Grain

_Plant in blocks (not rows) for pollination. Heat-loving._

### 🥒 Cucumber
**Category:** Vine

_Warm-season. Trellis vines for clean fruit. Needs consistent water._

### 🌿 Dill
**Category:** Herb

_Direct sow only. Self-sows readily. Companion to brassicas and tomatoes._

**Category:** Fruit Vegetable

_Heat-loving. Stake or cage — heavy fruit. Glossy skin indicates ripe._

### 🧄 Garlic
**Category:** Allium

**Category:** Leafy

**Category:** Leafy

**Category:** Flower

**Category:** Herb

_Vigorous spreader — contain in pots. Perennial. Cut back hard each fall._

**Category:** Fruit Vegetable
