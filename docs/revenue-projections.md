1. **Bundle uplift:** Bundle buyers have 2.3x LTV vs single buyers.
2. **Subscription stickiness:** At $4.99/mo, target 6-month retention = 60%.
3. **Physical upsell:** Add Printful POD at $14.99-$19.99 to increase AOV.
4. **SEO:** "plant journal" and "indoor plant tracker" keywords (low competition).
